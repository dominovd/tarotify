    How Yes or No Tarot Works

    Each of the 78 tarot cards carries an inherent energy — some lean toward expansion and affirmation (yes), others toward caution and blockage (no), and several occupy a liminal space of "not yet" or "it depends" (maybe).

    When you draw a card for a yes/no question, the card's archetype and your current energy interact to surface an answer from your subconscious. Think of it less as a definitive prediction and more as a mirror for your intuition.

    For the clearest reading: hold your question clearly in mind, breathe, and draw when you feel ready. The first card is always the right one.
